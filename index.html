Class of 1998 25th Reunions Service Organization Activity Appeal

WHO

Our Kids Read (https://www.ourkidsreadinc.org), a non-profit started by a group of Class of ‘98 and ‘99 Tigers, is one of this year’s service organizations for the Class of 1998’s 25th reunion

What

We’re bringing 50 2nd and 3rd grade students from Trenton to Princeton’s campus for a campus tour, a storytelling and drumming event and a literacy activity where children get three (3) free books (and a ton of Princeton swag) to take home with them

When:

Thursday, May 25th, 10 AM – 2 PM (during the first day of Princeton reunions this year)

WHERE

Whitman College

HELP WE NEED:

Volunteers

We need TONS of volunteers! We will need help the day of the event, setting up the book tables, reading to children in breakout rooms and other set-up and break-down activities (planning help leading up to the event is also welcome!). Please contact Jahmal Lake, OKR Executive Director, if you'd like to volunteer, by email, jahmal.lake@ourkidsreadinc.org, or by cell phone, 917-686-.3629

Book Donations

We are always in need of books donations, here is a list of books that we'd like to give to the students during their reunions experience: https://www.amazon.com/hz/wishlist/ls/2BV23IZL59S8A?ref_=wl_share

Sponsorship

We are in need of event sponsors, our total budget for the event is $15K to cover the cost of the bus charter, purchase of books to donate to the students, cost of lunch and the cost of the storytellers/performers. Here is a link to make a tax-deductible contribution: https://www.ourkidsreadinc.org/make-a-donation
